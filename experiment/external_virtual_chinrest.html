Now, let’s quickly test how far away you are sitting.

You might know that vision tests at a doctor’s practice often involve chinrests; the doctor basically asks you to sit away from a screen in a specific distance.
We do this here with a “virtual chinrest”.
Please get comfortable and then try not to move your head for the rest of the experiment.

Instructions

1. Put your finger on space bar on the keyboard.

2. Close your right eye. (Tip: it might be easier to cover your right eye by hand!)

3. Using your left eye, focus on the black square.

4. Click the button below to start the animation of the red ball. The red ball will disappear as it moves from right to left.
Press the “Space” key as soon as the ball disappears from your eye sight.


Please do it six times. Keep your right eye closed and hit the “Space” key fast when the ball disappears!
